FAQs

General Questions

What is blockchain-based certificate generation and validation?

Blockchain-based certificate generation and validation is a system where certificates are issued and verified using blockchain technology. This ensures the integrity and authenticity of certificates.

How does it work?

When a certificate is generated, its details are recorded on a blockchain, creating a tamper-proof record. To validate a certificate, its details are checked against the data stored on the blockchain.

Technical Questions

Which blockchain platform is used?

The website uses Ethereum blockchain for certificate issuance and validation.

Are the certificates secure?

Yes, certificates stored on the blockchain cannot be altered or tampered with, ensuring their security and authenticity.
